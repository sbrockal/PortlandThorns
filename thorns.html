The Portland Thorns FC is an American professional women's soccer team based in Portland, Oregon. Established in 2012, the team began play in 2013 in the then-eight-team National Women's Soccer League (NWSL). The club won the NWSL Shield in 2016 and a second NWSL Championship in 2017. In 2020, they won the Community Shield with the best record in the 2020 NWSL Fall Series. In 2021, the Thorns won the NWSL Challenge Cup, the Women's International Champions Cup, and the NWSL Shield. They followed up in 2022 by winning the NWSL championship.

Meet the Team

Sophia Smith

Sophia Smith is a fast and skillful forward known for her ability to score goals in critical moments. She has become a key player for the Thorns and the U.S. Women's National Team with her versatility and creativity on the field. Her rapid development has made her one of the brightest young stars in women's soccer.

Christine Sinclair

Christine Sinclair is the all-time leading goal scorer in international soccer, for both men and women. A veteran forward and captain, she brings immense experience, leadership, and precision to the Portland Thorns. Sinclair is respected globally for her vision, football IQ, and ability to perform under pressure.

Morgan Weaver

Morgan Weaver is a determined forward with a fierce playing style, known for her relentless work ethic. Her speed and ability to take on defenders have made her a valuable asset to the Thorns. Weaver has consistently impressed with her offensive contributions and high-energy performances.

Becky Sauerbrunn

Becky Sauerbrunn is a legendary center-back known for her leadership, defensive prowess, and calmness under pressure. As captain of the U.S. Women's National Team, she has won multiple international titles and is a role model for young defenders. Her tactical awareness and ability to organize the backline are crucial to the Thorns' success.

Bella Bixby

Bella Bixby is the Thorns' first-choice goalkeeper, known for her sharp reflexes and command of the penalty area. She has made several game-saving stops, proving her reliability between the posts. Bixby's strong presence and confidence have quickly made her a fan favorite.

Hina Sugita

Hina Sugita is a creative midfielder with excellent ball control and vision. She is known for her ability to distribute the ball and create scoring opportunities for her teammates. Sugita's technical skills and quick decision-making make her a dynamic playmaker for the Thorns.

Jesse Flemming

Jesse Flemming is a composed midfielder with a high soccer IQ and the ability to control the pace of the game. Her precise passing and tactical awareness have helped her excel both for the Thorns and on the international stage with Canada. Flemming’s versatility makes her a key player in the midfield..

Marie Müller

Marie Müller is a hardworking midfielder with a strong defensive presence and excellent stamina. Known for her ability to break up opposition attacks and transition quickly, she plays a vital role in maintaining balance in the Thorns' midfield. Müller’s work rate and discipline make her a reliable team player.

Meghan Klingenberg

Meghan Klingenberg is an experienced defender known for her incredible crossing ability and attacking support from the left flank. A veteran of the U.S. Women's National Team, she brings leadership and tactical intelligence to the Thorns. Klingenberg’s energy and consistency on both ends of the field are pivotal to the team’s success.

Olivia Moultrie

Olivia Moultrie is a rising star and one of the youngest players to sign a professional contract in the NWSL. Known for her technical skills and creativity, Moultrie has already shown great promise as a midfielder. Her quick feet and sharp passing make her a constant threat in attack.
